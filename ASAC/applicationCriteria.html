Admission

Admission criteria and application deadlines.

Please note that the criteria of each course (scholarship) varies and depends on the assigned project criteria.
You can find below the common admissions criteria:

1- Age is usually between 18-35.

2- Nationality is either Jordanians and Refugees or both.

3- Applicant must have at least a bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, IT, or any other related fields. We can also accept Diploma students in the same fields depending on the assigned project criteria.

4- Unemployed or part-time employee with no social security.

5- Minimum English Level: (B2).

As for the application deadlines, please note that the deadline depends on the number of students target (when the target is met), and the duration of each project.
