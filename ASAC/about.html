About Us

Our Perpose

ASAC is dedicated to empowering a new generation of tech innovators and leaders.

Our mission is to provide cutting-edge education in advanced computing and IT, Fostering an environment of innovation, critical thinking, and practical skills development.

we strive to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry needs, ensuring our graduates are ready to contribute significantly to the global technology sector and drive positive social change through Technolgy.

Vision

Our vision at ASAC is to set a benchmark in computer science education, aligning with Luminus Life Plus's commitment to fostering regional prosperity.

We aim to be at the forefront of developing proficient professionals who are not only ready for today's challenges but are also driving forces for tomorrow's innovations, contributing to the creation of substantial and decent employment opportunities.

Our Mission

ASAC, an integral part of Luminus Life Plus, is dedicated to cultivating a skilled workforce ready to thrive in the global tech industry.

Our mission is to advance education in computing and technology, aligning with Luminus Life Plus's goal to create 250,000 regional job opportunities, thus driving sustainable development and economic growth in the MENA region.
