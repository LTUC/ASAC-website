Who We Are ?

About

Luminus Technical University's Abdul Aziz Al Ghurair School offers Cloud Computing programs, including a Bachelor's degree, a two-year BTEC diploma, and software development courses to meet the demand for skilled professionals.

Vision

Our vision is to globally empower millions through affordable lifelong learning and wellness programs, using a hybrid model for both on-the-ground and online delivery.

Mission

Our mission is to globally empower individuals through affordable and diverse educational programs, integrating practical education, wellness initiatives, and cutting-edge technology for lifelong employability and well-being.

ASAC Campus

Experience innovation at our campus! Join a vibrant community, cutting-edge facilities, and a supportive environment. Elevate your education with us – where learning meets inspiration
