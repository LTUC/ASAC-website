About the department

The Abdulaziz Al Ghurair Department of Computing (ASAC) was established in 2019 to build the programming language and development skills and capabilities of learners while qualifying them for current and future employment in the fields.

Modern programs are designed based on extensive studies of oil prices in global markets and excellence in them.

The initial stages of spatial design went through a phase of focused collaborative discussion, interaction and a sense of community. Our education partner, Classmates Academy, provided more insight into ideas on developing an environment that encourages learning and growth. Implemented Architecture, which also designed and built Expedia's Jordan office, also introduced best and best practices in working at ASAC as technology companies that motivate students to be an impactful and powerful team.
